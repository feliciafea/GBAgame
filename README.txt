Ladybug game goal: eat the skittles before time runs out.

CONTROLS:
Enter - start
Up - move up
down - move down
right - move right
left - move left
Select/backspace - restart

RULES:
Level 1
Move the lady bug to touch the red skittles to increase the score. 
Get a score of 3 in before 30 seconds passes to get to the next level

Level 2:
Get a score of 8 before 20 seconds passes to win.
